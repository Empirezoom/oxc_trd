DATA PROTECTION

Protocol v2.4 | Last Revision: April 12, 2026

SECURITY IS NOT OPTIONAL. At OCTALX, we treat your data with the same institutional rigor as our financial assets. This Privacy Protocol outlines how your information is harvested, shielded, and managed within our ecosystem.

01 INFORMATION HARVESTING

We collect specific data points required to maintain structural integrity and regulatory compliance:

  • Identity: Username, Nationality, DOB
  • Contact: Email and Secure Comm Channels
  • KYC: Encrypted ID Documents
  • Financial: Wallet Nodes and Ledger Data

02 STRATEGIC USAGE

Your harvested data is utilized exclusively for platform optimization, identity verification under AML/KYC regulations, and multi-factor security enforcement. We do not participate in third-party data monetization.

03 ENCRYPTED STORAGE

Identity documents and sensitive data reside in isolated, encrypted vaults with air-gapped backups. Access is restricted to Tier-1 compliance officers and is audited in real-time by our automated security systems.

04 DATA SOVEREIGNTY

You maintain ultimate sovereignty over your data. Upon request and verification, you may access, export, or request the permanent deletion of your PII (Personally Identifiable Information) from our active systems, subject to legal retention obligations.

05 TERMINAL SECURITY

All interactions with the OctalX terminal are secured via SSL/TLS 1.3 encryption protocols. We recommend the use of hardware-based 2FA (Two-Factor Authentication) for all high-value account accounts.
